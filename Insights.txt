Sales Performance Insights – Superstore Dashboard

1]Overall Sales Trends
Year-over-year sales show steady growth.
Seasonal spikes observed in November–December.

2]Category Performance
Technology generates the highest revenue.
Office Supplies have high volume but low profit.
Furniture has mixed performance due to high discounts.

3]Regional Insights
West region leads in total sales.
South performs weaker but has high-value customers.

4]Customer Insights
Top 10 customers contribute significantly to total revenue.
High repeat purchases in Corporate and Consumer segments.

5]Profitability
Profit ratio strongest in Technology category.
Losses mainly come from high discount furniture items.

6]Recommendations
Reduce discounts in Furniture to improve profit.
Increase marketing for low-performing regions.
Grow Tech category — highest ROI.
